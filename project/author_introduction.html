Author Introduction

Dr. Li Wei

Principal Investigator | Professor of Materials Chemistry

Dr. Li Wei is a Professor of Materials Chemistry at the Institute of Advanced Materials, University of Science and Technology. Her research focuses on the design, synthesis, and application of functional nanomaterials for catalytic and energy-related applications. She has published over 80 peer-reviewed articles in journals such as Advanced Materials, ACS Catalysis, and Green Chemistry. Dr. Li received her Ph.D. in Chemistry from Tsinghua University and completed postdoctoral research at MIT.

Zhang Ming

Ph.D. Candidate

Zhang Ming is a third-year Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Chemistry. His research interests include the synthesis of transition metal oxide nanocomposites and their applications in sustainable organic synthesis. This work forms part of his doctoral dissertation. He received his B.S. in Applied Chemistry from Nanjing University.

Wang Jia

Research Assistant

Wang Jia is a Research Assistant in the Materials Chemistry Laboratory. She specializes in materials characterization using X-ray diffraction, electron microscopy, and surface analysis techniques. She received her M.S. in Materials Science from Shanghai Jiao Tong University.

Chen Xiao

Undergraduate Research Intern

Chen Xiao is an undergraduate student majoring in Chemistry. She joined the research group in 2023 and has been involved in catalyst synthesis and preliminary activity testing. She is currently pursuing her B.S. degree at the University of Science and Technology.

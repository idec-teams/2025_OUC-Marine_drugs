Lab Notebook

Date: March 15, 2024

Experimenter: Zhang Ming

Experiment: Synthesis of Co₃O₄-ZnO nanocomposites (Batch #CZ-001)

Procedure: Followed Protocol 1 with slight modifications: reaction temperature 160°C, time 12 hours.

Observations: Purple precipitate formed immediately upon addition of NaOH solution. After hydrothermal treatment, the precipitate turned dark brown. Yield: 0.187 g (85% based on metal precursors).

Next Steps: Characterize sample using XRD and TEM. Prepare for catalytic testing.

Date: March 17, 2024

Experimenter: Zhang Ming

Experiment: XRD and TEM characterization of CZ-001

Results: XRD pattern shows peaks corresponding to Co₃O₄ and ZnO with no impurities. TEM images show nanoparticles with average size ~12 nm. Good dispersion observed.

Comments: Synthesis appears successful. Proceed with catalytic activity testing.

Date: March 19, 2024

Experimenter: Wang Jia

Experiment: Catalytic activity test of CZ-001 in Suzuki-Miyaura reaction

Conditions: 0.5 mol% catalyst, 25°C, ethanol/water (5:1), 2 hours

Results: Conversion: 98%, Selectivity to biphenyl: 96%

Observations: Reaction mixture turned from clear to dark during the reaction. Catalyst could be easily separated by centrifugation.

Date: March 21, 2024

Experimenter: Chen Xiao

Experiment: Catalyst recyclability test (Cycle 1-5)

Results:

  • Cycle 1: 98% conversion
  • Cycle 2: 96% conversion
  • Cycle 3: 94% conversion
  • Cycle 4: 93% conversion
  • Cycle 5: 91% conversion

Comments: Excellent catalyst stability observed over 5 cycles. Minimal loss of activity.

Date: March 25, 2024

Experimenter: Zhang Ming

Experiment: XPS analysis of fresh and used catalyst (after 5 cycles)

Results: No significant changes in oxidation states of Co and Zn observed. Surface composition remains consistent.

Conclusion: Catalyst maintains its chemical state during reaction, confirming good stability.

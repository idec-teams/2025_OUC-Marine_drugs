Dr. Li Wei

Principal Investigator

Dr. Li Wei is a Professor of Materials Chemistry at the Institute of Advanced Materials, University of Science and Technology. With over 15 years of research experience in functional materials, his work focuses on the synthesis and application of novel catalysts for environmental remediation.

He has published more than 80 peer-reviewed papers in journals such as Nature Materials and ACS Nano, and holds 12 patents related to catalytic material design. Dr. Li leads a research team of 15 members, including postdocs, PhD students, and research assistants.
